# GridView Logo — Marlboro-Inspired × Minimal Tech

## Design Philosophy

**Marlboro's confidence + modern data product cleanliness.**

This isn't busy or aggressive. It's sophisticated and confident. The design breathes.

## Icon Concept

A minimal **2×2 grid** where the bottom squares are subtly offset inward, creating a chevron suggestion through negative space alone. No extra shapes, no clutter—just smart arrangement.

- **Top row**: Two squares, aligned
- **Bottom row**: Two squares, offset inward ~5px
- **Result**: Implied downward chevron, Marlboro heritage

## Color Philosophy

Red and white are PRIMARY. Black is accent only.

| Color | Hex | Role |
|-------|-----|------|
| Marlboro Red | `#EE1C25` | Primary brand color |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Primary on dark, background on light |
| Dark | `#1a1a1a` | Accent only (backgrounds, text on light) |

### Color Usage by Context

**Dark backgrounds**: Red + white icon, white/red wordmark  
**Light backgrounds**: All-red icon, dark/red wordmark

## Typography

- **Font**: System sans-serif (SF Pro, Segoe UI, etc.)
- **Weight**: 600 (semi-bold, not heavy)
- **Letter-spacing**: Generous (+2-3px)
- **Treatment**: "Grid" neutral, "View" in Marlboro red

## Design Principles

1. **Let it breathe** — Generous spacing, no crowding
2. **Red & white first** — Black only where necessary
3. **Subtle, not obvious** — Chevron implied, not drawn
4. **Confidence over aggression** — Premium, not extreme sports
5. **Timeless** — Should look good in 10 years
